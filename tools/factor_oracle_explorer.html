Seeker Integration Notes

-- The oracle operates on beat slices, not individual notes.
-- Each slice = all events within one beat.
-- Equivalence = same pitch classes, ignoring rhythm.
-- Generation concatenates whole beat slices,
-- preserving internal timing automatically.

-- Params: fidelity (0-100), length (beats to generate)
-- Triangle-wave entropy envelope from Dialogue
-- controls per-beat mutation depth after oracle output.

Based on Allauzen, Crochemore & Raffinot (1999), Assayag & Dubnov (OMax), and Nika & Chemillier (ImproteK, IRCAM).
